    TV FAQs

    Featured FAQs

    What is WOW! OnDemand and how do I get it?

    WOW! OnDemand is our Video OnDemand service that allows WOW! Digital Cable customers the ability to select from hundreds of movies and other programs and watch them when they want. WOW! OnDemand includes a variety of programming including movies, kids’ and family shows, events, music, sports and more.

    With WOW! OnDemand, you can enjoy DVD-like functionality to rewind, fast forward and pause programs. Customers that have WOW! Digital Cable service and a Digital receiver, DVR, HD receiver, or HD DVR receiver will have access to WOW! OnDemand.

    How do I turn on Parental Controls?

    Parental Controls can be managed from either the settings category on your ULTRA connected TV or by going online to My Account. The first time Parental Controls are used, a PIN will need to be created. In the Settings category, select Parental Controls. Enter a four-digit PIN on your remote and then re-enter the PIN to confirm.

    How do I find the ULTRA TV App and log in?

    The ULTRA TV App can be downloaded from both Apple iTunes and the Google Play Store by searching for "WOW! ULTRA." Once downloaded, use your WOW! username and password to log in. The ULTRA App is supported on devices running iOS 5 or greater and most Android devices.

    How do I sign in to Netflix?

    If you are already a member of Netflix, select Sign in. Enter your email address and password. Once you have signed in you can begin to use your Netflix account on ULTRA. You will not need to sign in again. (*Not available in all areas)

    How can I sign out of Netflix on ULTRA?

    From the Smart Menu use the left arrow on the remote to highlight the Settings card. Press the up or down arrows to highlight "Netflix," press "OK" on the remote, and press "OK" on "Deactivate Netflix." If you deactivate the device, you will need to log in again the next time you want to watch Netflix on ULTRA. (*Not available in all areas)

    Where can I find a list of channels available in my area?

    Click here to view and print your channel lineup.

    How does ULTRA Whole-Home DVR work?

    A Whole-Home HD DVR allows you to watch and manage all of your recorded shows from any ULTRA connected TV. To record a program, select the "Record" button from your remote. To record a series, select the "Info" button on the remote to access the series recording options.

    What do I do if I am having issues with my WOW! video service? (Picture Quality, Picture Distortion, Black Screen, Video OnDemand or my DVR.)

    Make sure the wiring to the television set is correct and connections are tight. Connections should be hand tightened. Reset your cable box by unplugging it from electricity for 1 minute.  After plugging it back in, wait for the time to display before turning the box back on (this may take 5-7 minutes).

    If you are still having issues with your WOW! video service, please note any error codes and contact us.

    How do I watch Netflix?

    You can access Netflix from the ULTRA horizontal menu or channel 1500. If you are an existing Netflix subscriber, log in with your username and password. If you are not an existing Netflix subscriber, you can start your free trial by setting up a membership right on your ULTRA connected TV. You will need to establish a Netflix subscription by creating an account and entering your credit card number. All account management and billing is direct with Netflix, so to cancel you must contact Netflix directly.
